About Us

1080 Brew

1080Brew started when Marian and Melissa realized the tenants at Rockwall Studios definitely needed their own local supply of caffeine in the building! 1080Brew is a cute and cozy coffee shop serving up Toby's Estate brand coffee and espresso! We offer our customers pastries from Balthazar and Colson's and seriously delicious made-to-order sandwiches! Our coffee is the best in the neighborhood and so is our service! ;D Check out our menu and come pay us a visit! We look forward to serving you!
