Learn About 1080 Project Space

Our Gallery space is a 1500 sf space designed for the purpose of offering our tenants an in-house place to exhibit their work. When our tenants are not utilizing the space we envision it becoming a go-to location for Art Exhibits, Performance Art, Work Shops, Markets and more! With its 23ft ceiling height, the sky is the limit! Pass through the hallway next to the Gallery and you enter The Deep End Bar and Restaurant. The owners of this restaurant are all too happy to offer food and beverage service to all events going on in 1080 Project Space! Give us a call to check it out and book your next event with us!
